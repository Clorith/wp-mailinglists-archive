CONFERENCE DETAILS
==================

The Fourth International Conference on Digital Information Processing, 
Data Mining and Wireless Communication (DIPDMWC2017)

Universitas Mercu Buana ? Meruya Campus, Jakarta, Indonesia
July 19-20, 2017

http://sdiwc.net/conferences/4th-international-conference-on-digital-information-processing-data-mining-and-wireless-communication/

The event will be held over two days, with presentations delivered by 
researchers from the international community, including presentations 
from keynote speakers and state-of-the-art lectures.


TOPICS
======

The conference welcomes papers on the following (but not limited to) 
research topics:

- Image Processing
- Modulation, Coding, and Channel Analysis
- Bioinformatics & Biomedical Imaging
- Natural Language Processing
- Computer-Aided Surgery
- Speech Recognition, Analysis and Synthesis
- Feature Extraction and Data Reduction
- Optimization Techniques
- Text and Web Mining
- Data Mining for Network/Cyber Security
- Data Mining for Climate Change and Impacts
- Bluetooth and Personal Area Networks
- Mobile Management in Wireless Networks
- IP Multimedia Sub-Systems
- Mobile/ Wireless Network Modeling and Simulation
- Wireless Network Standard and Protocols


PUBLICATION
===========

All the submitted papers will be reviewed by a minimum of two reviewers, 
and all the registered papers will be submitted to IEEE for potential 
inclusion to IEEE Xplore as well as other Abstracting and Indexing (A&I) 
databases.
